Demo 2 — portal · BYOA, the portal drives

This is the portal. Notice there is nowhere to enter AWS credentials — that's the point. The portal has its own identity; it assumes a role in your account and launches compute there. Those instances run under the portal's role, not yours: you reach them only through this portal, never directly. spored still registers DNS with infra and self-terminates on its TTL.

Launch (into your connected account, via the portal)

Portal-managed instances

These live in your account but are owned by the portal's role. The only actions offered are portal-mediated — there is no direct SSH affordance, because you don't hold the credential that controls the box.

Terminal (brokered by the portal)

This shell is opened by the portal via SSM — the portal called StartSession and handed your browser only a session token, never AWS credentials. You couldn't open this yourself.
